# 平抛运动性质

## 基础

$\Delta v = gt$

水平:

$ \left\{
\begin{aligned}
v_x &= v_0 \\
x &= v_0 t
\end{aligned}
\right.
$

竖直:

$ \left\{
\begin{aligned}
v_y &= gt \\
y &= \frac{1}{2} g t^2 \\
\end{aligned}
\right.
$

合成:

$ \left\{
\begin{aligned}
v &= \sqrt{v_x^2 + v_y^2} \\
x &= \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \\
\end{aligned}
\right.
$

## 推论

$
\because
\left\{
\begin{aligned}
\tan{\theta} &= \dfrac{v_y}{v_x} = \dfrac{2y_A}{x_A} \\
\tan{\alpha} &= \dfrac{y_A}{x_A}
\end{aligned}
\right.
$

$
\therefore
\tan{\theta} = 2 \tan{\alpha}

$\tan{\theta} = 2 \tan{\alpha}$

$x_b = \frac{1}{2} x_a$
