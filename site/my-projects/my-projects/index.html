My projects

LexiGen¶

LexiGen is an open-source fill-in-the-blank problems generator using local AI with Zero Cost.

Ollama Writing Tools¶

Ollama Writing Tools, is a Windows application that enhances your writing with AI-powered assistance through Ollama. Simply select text anywhere, and get instant writing improvements with a single click. It is 100% free and open source, and even customizable, you can configure different prompts for different writing tasks.

(Raycast Extension) Lyric Fever Control¶

Lyric Fever Control, is an extension for Lyric Fever(1), an app that provides "Ultimate Spotify & Apple Music Lyrics experience for macOS".
